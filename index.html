A Wellness Center for Pets

Our Mission

Our mission at Animal Rehabilitation & Wellness is to provide excellent professional rehabilitation and wellness services for your pet. Our facility is uniquely designed to allow us to provide a broad range of therapeutic exercise in an open and stimulating environment.

Less Stress

Because we do not perform other medical services or painful procedures, animals experience less stress and are more willing to engage in the activities at our center. Most look forward to their "play" sessions and the relief from pain that they feel after a day with us.

Exercise Therapy

Exercise therapy or rehabilitation therapy for animals is equivalent to human physical therapy. There are many aspects to animal rehabilitation and exercise therapy, each which offers a particular benefit. Every patient's program is individually designed for their illness.

About Animal Rehabilitation & Wellness, Inc.

Clinical Director Cory Sims has been practicing veterinary medicine for 8 years. Operations Manager Troy Mendia is a veterinary assistant with specific training in animal rehabilitation and therapeutic medicine.

All of the staff at Animal Rehabilitation & Wellness are passionate about providing non-invasive, drug-free therapies to compliment and enhance the benefits of clinical medicine.

We welcome inquiries about our services and the benefits of rehabilitation therapy for animals.
